# Why SlipperOS

SlipperOS was born from a conversation between two friends — Loki and Seal.

The idea was simple: run our own kernel inside the OC2r mod. Not a Linux port, not
"yet another distribution" — but our own, from boot to shell. Write everything from
scratch and understand every byte.

Loki brought Rust and a love for minimalism. Seal brought the OC2r mod and the
understanding of how this should work on RISC-V. Together, we built something
neither of us could have done alone.

SlipperOS is:
- Minimal abstractions
- Full control
- Understanding every byte

Oh, and there's a seal.

## Why not Linux

Linux for embedded systems is 20 million lines and a config with a thousand options.
SlipperOS is a few thousand lines and one Cargo.toml.

Not for production. For understanding.
